3D MASSAGE
MECHANISM
The Osaki 3D Signature II features a precise 3D massage mechanism designed to deliver accurate and adjustable pressure. Advanced massage rollers move smoothly along the track to provide a more targeted and personalized massage experience.
SL-TRACK
SYSTEM
The SL-Track system follows the natural curve of your spine, guiding the massage rollers from the neck and shoulders down through the lower back and hips for smooth, full-body coverage. The ergonomic track design ensures consistent pressure and a more comfortable, natural massage experience.
20-CELL FULL-BODY
AIR MASSAGE
With 20 strategically placed air cells throughout the chair, the Osaki 3D Signature II delivers soothing compression therapy that helps improve circulation and relieve muscle tension. The intelligent Auto Body Scan system detects your body shape to provide a more accurate and personalized massage experience.
SPACE-SAVING
TECHNOLOGY
With Space-Saving Technology, the Osaki 3D Signature II requires only 5 inches of clearance from the wall while still achieving a full recline.
SPRING-LOADED
EXTENDABLE FOOTREST
The Osaki 3D Signature II 's footrest extends up to 5.2 inches with a spring-loaded mechanism, easily accommodating users of different heights.
ROLLER & PLATE
FOOT MASSAGE
The Osaki 3D Signature II uses foot rollers and acupressure plates beneath the feet to stimulate reflex zones and acupressure points, delivering a deeper, targeted foot massage.
ZERO GRAVITY
RECLINING
The Osaki 3D Signature II 's zero gravity technology creates a weightless recline that evenly supports your body, easing back pressure and elevating comfort. The handheld remote offers convenient 1-stage Zero Gravity, while the mobile app allows you to select between 2-stage Zero Gravity positions for enhanced relaxation.
HEATING
THERAPY

The Osaki 3D Signature II delivers soothing heat therapy to the lumbar area, helping to relax tight muscles and enhance circulation.

*The heating therapy provides warmth in the range of approximately 75 - 83°F.
CONTROLLER
ORGANIZER
The Osaki 3D Signature II includes a built-in controller organizer, allowing you to neatly store the handheld remote and keep your space tidy.
EMERGENCY
STOP BUTTON
The Osaki 3D Signature II includes a dedicated emergency stop button, allowing you to immediately stop the massage operation whenever needed for enhanced safety.
ANTI-PINCH
PROTECTION
The power box cover automatically detects nearby obstacles to help prevent pinching and improve safety.

Operate the Signature II with ease using either the mobile app or the wireless remote. The app provides advanced customization and quick access to massage programs, while the remote allows simple and direct adjustments during your massage session.

• If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
