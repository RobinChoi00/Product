Elevate your relaxation experience with the Forma Massage Chair. Designed with intelligent wellness technology, it combines Intelligent Health Detection, a precision 3D + 2D massage mechanism, Zero Gravity recline, and Intelligent Voice Control to deliver a truly personalized massage. From targeted tension relief to full-body relaxation, the Forma creates a smarter, more immersive wellness experience tailored to you.
INTELLIGENT
HEALTH DETECTION
The Intelligent Health Detection system analyzes your blood oxygen, heart rate, and fatigue levels in real time to recommend a personalized massage program for targeted relief and enhanced relaxation.
3D+2D MASSAGE
MECHANISM
The advanced 3D+2D massage mechanism combines the depth and flexibility of 3D upper rollers with the precision of 2D lower rollers for a balanced full-body massage experience. The 3D rollers adjust outward to deliver deeper, more human-like relief along the neck, shoulders, and back, while the 2D rollers provide consistent, targeted coverage through the lower back and lumbar area.
INTELLIGENT
VOICE CONTROL
Enjoy effortless relaxation with Intelligent Voice Control, designed to make your massage experience completely hands-free. Simply speak your commands to change massage modes and entire zero gravity — delivering seamless comfort and personalized control with just the sound of your voice.
BODY SCAN
TECHNOLOGY
Body Scan Technology intelligently maps your body’s shape, height, and pressure points to deliver a fully personalized massage experience. By detecting key areas like your neck and shoulders, the chair automatically adjusts massage rollers and settings for targeted comfort, deeper relief, and a massage that feels custom-made for you.
SL-TRACK
SYSTEM
The advanced SL-Track System is designed to follow the natural curve of your spine, extending massage coverage from your neck to your glutes. By delivering continuous, precision-targeted relief along the body, it creates a deeper, more immersive massage experience that feels both natural and restorative.
ZERO GRAVITY
RECLINING
Inspired by NASA technology, the Forma offers Zero Gravity that gently positions the body into a near-weightless state, helping reduce pressure on the spine while promoting deeper relaxation. This position provides better circulation and a weightless, stress-free massage experience.
SPACE-SAVING
TECHNOLOGY
Crafted for modern living, the Forma features advanced Space-Saving Technology that slides the chair forward as it reclines, needing just 6 inches of wall clearance. Enjoy a luxurious zero-gravity recline and immersive full-body relaxation — all without sacrificing valuable space in your home.
26-CELL AIR
COMPRESSION MASSAGE
The Forma features an advanced 26-cell air compression massage system that uses strategically positioned air chambers to gently compress and release key areas of the body, replicating the feel of a professional hands-on massage. This rhythmic pressure helps ease muscle tension, promote healthy circulation, and provide a soothing full-body relaxation experience.
CALF & BACK
HEATING THERAPY
The Forma's heating therapy provides soothing, targeted warmth designed to relax tired calves and lower back while helping release built-up muscle tension. By encouraging improved circulation and deeper relaxation, heat transforms every session into an elevated level of comfort to your massage experience.
* The heating therapy feature warms in the range of approx. 75°F – 83°F

MINIMUM DOORWAY & PACKAGING
Measuring Guide
• Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 1 inch from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1/2 inch from each side)
• Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
• If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
• If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.
