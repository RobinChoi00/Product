Titan Gemini
24-CELL FULL BODY
AIR MASSAGE
The Titan Gemini features 24 strategically placed air cells that gently envelop the shoulders, arms, legs, and feet for a comprehensive full body massage. Designed to deliver rhythmic compression, the system helps relieve built-up tension, improve circulation, and promote deeper relaxation.
FULL BODY SYNC
MASSAGE MECH
The Titan Gemini features 18 synchronized 2D massage heads that work in unison to precisely target the back. This integrated system delivers a deep, consistent massage that maintains balanced pressure from top to bottom, helping the body relax, release tension, and recover more efficiently.
SPECIALIZED
NECK MASSAGE
Titan Gemini features a specialized neck massage system with dual rollers moving in opposite directions to gently stretch and release both sides of the neck. This innovative motion provides a deeper, more targeted massage that helps ease stiffness, reduce built-up tension, and restore natural flexibility for lasting comfort.
STRAIGHT TRACK &
GLUTES KNEADING
The Titan Gemini’s straight track system allows its massage rollers to glide smoothly from the neck down to the hips, delivering steady, full-length relief along the spine. Dedicated kneading rollers also target the glutes, enhancing lower body comfort and overall relaxation.
HEATING SYSTEM
The Titan Gemini delivers soothing heat therapy to the back, waist, and calves, helping to relax tight muscles and enhance circulation. Gentle warmth melts away built-up tension and deepens relaxation, creating a more complete and restorative massage experience.
*The heating therapy provides warmth in the range of approximately 75 - 83°F.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 1 inch from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1/2 inch from each side)
