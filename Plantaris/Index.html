The Plantaris 4D Massage Chair brings elevated, everyday relaxation into your home with fluid 4D massage technology that feels natural, deep, and deeply restorative. Hands-free voice control lets you customize your massage effortlessly, while the rotating footrest eases stiffness. Paired with deep calf kneading to melt away tension, Plantaris is designed to help you unwind fully, recharge your body, and settle into total comfort—whenever you need it.
INTELLIGENT VOICE CONTROL
The Plantaris 4D features intelligent voice control for fully hands-free operation—simply say "Switch to Voice Mode" to start your massage with an effortless voice command.
EMERGENCY
STOP BUTTON
Standing out from other chair models, the Plantaris 4D holds an emergency stop button to halt the massage session entirely once pressed.
ROCKING MASSAGE POSITION
The chair's gentle rocking sways the body in a fluid, cradle-like rhythm, quieting the nervous system, releasing tension, and drawing you into deeper relaxation.
360° ROTATING
FOOTREST
The Plantaris 4D footrest features 360° rotation— clockwise, counterclockwise, and alternating—to help ease leg stiffness and promote healthy circulation.
SPECIALIZED
FOOT MASSAGERS
The footrest's rotating massage discs apply thumb-like pressure to the feet to stretch tight muscles, activate reflexology points, and improve circulation.
DEEP CALF
KNEADING
Going beyond standard massage chairs, the Plantaris 4D applies focused deep-kneading massage to the calves to reduce muscle tension, stimulate circulation, and aid recovery.

& BUMPERS

MECHANISM

BODY SCAN
TECHNOLOGY

2-STAGE
ZERO GRAVITY RECLINING
Enjoy a weightless state of relaxation with the Plantaris 4D's two-stage Zero Gravity Technology, which evenly supports your body as the chair reclines to reduce back pressure and enhance overall comfort.
SL-TRACK
SYSTEM
The SL-track is designed to mirror the natural curve of your spine, extending from the neck down to the upper glutes to deliver a seamless, full-length back massage.
SPACE-SAVING
TECHNOLOGY
The space-saving design allows the chair to glide forward as it reclines, reaching a full recline with 7 inches wall clearance required.
24-CELL
AIR COMPRESSION
MASSAGE
Equipped with 24 strategically positioned air cells, the Plantaris 4D delivers targeted, gentle compression to ease tension, enhance relaxation, and support overall wellness.
LUMBAR
HEATING THERAPY
Gentle lumbar heating helps the Plantaris 4D relieve tension and deepen your feeling of relaxation.
