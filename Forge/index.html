Forge is a powerful 3D massage chair engineered to shape relief with precision. Inspired by the strength of forging, its adjustable 3D rollers deliver deep, targeted pressure that breaks down tension and restores comfort. Built for performance and crafted for relaxation, Forge transforms pressure into total-body relief.
3D MASSAGE
MECHANISM
A 3D massage mechanism allows the rollers to move not only up and down and side to side, but also in and out, adjusting depth to deliver a more precise, human-like massage that targets muscles more effectively.
BODY SCAN
TECHNOLOGY
The intelligent body scan adapts the massage to your unique shape and contours, accurately positioning the rollers for a highly effective and exceptionally comfortable experience.
3-STAGE
ZERO GRAVITY RECLINING
Experience a weightless sense of relaxation with the Forge 3D's three-stage Zero Gravity Technology, designed to evenly support your body as the chair reclines, relieving back pressure and maximizing overall comfort.
SL-TRACK
SYSTEM
The SL-track follows the natural curvature of your spine, extending from the neck to the glutes to provide a smooth, full-length back massage.
SPACE-SAVING
TECHNOLOGY
The space-saving design lets the chair glide forward as it reclines, achieving a full recline while requiring only 3 inches of wall clearance.
24-CELL
AIR COMPRESSION
MASSAGE
With 24 strategically placed air cells, the Forge 3D provides targeted, gentle compression to relieve tension, deepen relaxation, and promote overall wellness.
LUMBAR
HEATING THERAPY
Soothing lumbar heat enhances the Forge 3D's ability to ease tension, amplifying your overall sense of relaxation.

* The heating therapy provides warmth in the range of approximately 75 - 83°F.

MINIMUM DOORWAY & PACKAGING
Measuring Guide
• Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 1 inch from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1/2 inch from each side)
• Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
• If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
• If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.
