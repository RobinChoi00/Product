Elevate your relaxation with a massage chair designed for a smarter, more personalized experience. The 3D+2D Dual Massage Mechanism delivers powerful, simultaneous upper and lower body massages for perfectly balanced relief. A dedicated ankle stretching mechanism gently rotates the footrest to loosen tight joints and refresh tired legs. With intelligent health detection that analyzes your body and hands-free voice control, every massage is effortlessly tailored to your needs for a truly modern wellness experience.
INTELLIGENT HEALTH DETECTION
The AI-powered health detection system scans key metrics—including heart rate, blood oxygen, microcirculation, and fatigue levels—to suggest two massage programs that may meet your body's real-time needs.
ANKLE STRETCHING
MECHANISM
Unlike typical massage chairs that overlook ankles, the Ignite Sync provides an ankle stretching mechanism that gently rotates the footrest back and forth. This creates targeted stretching motions that improve flexibility and relieve tension in the ankles.
3D + 2D
DUAL MASSAGE
MECHANISM
The 3D+2D Dual Massage Mechanism features two massage systems that work on the upper and lower body for a more comprehensive experience.
The upper mechanism offers adjustable intensity levels, allowing you to customize the massage for the upper body while the lower mechanism automatically adjusts and operates according to the selected massage mode.
INTELLIGENT
VOICE CONTROL
Intelligent voice control lets you operate the massage chair hands-free, effortlessly adjusting settings and programs using simple vocal commands.
BODY SCAN
TECHNOLOGY
The body scan technology precisely maps your body's natural contours, positioning the rollers for optimal alignment and delivering a smooth, deeply satisfying massage experience.
ZERO GRAVITY RECLINING
Inspired by NASA innovations, the Ignite Sync incorporates Zero Gravity Reclining technology, allowing you to recline in a near-weightless position that eases spinal pressure and maximizes relaxation.
SL-TRACK
SYSTEM
Designed to follow the natural curve of your spine, the SL-track extends from neck to glutes, providing a seamless, deeply satisfying full-back massage.
SPACE-SAVING
TECHNOLOGY
The space-saving design allows the chair to recline fully, while requiring minimal wall clearance of 3 inches, making it perfect for compact living spaces.
38-CELL
AIR COMPRESSION MASSAGE
Thirty-eight strategically placed air cells deliver a full-body air compression massage, gently enveloping your muscles to relieve tension and enhance circulation.
LUMBAR
HEATING THERAPY
Soothing lumbar heat gently warms tired muscles, helping to relieve tension, improve circulation, and deepen your overall sense of relaxation. *
* The heating therapy provides warmth in the range of approximately 75 - 83°F.

MINIMUM DOORWAY & PACKAGING
Measuring Guide
• Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 1 inch from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1/2 inch from each side)
• Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
• If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
• If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.
